Daily 5 Hours Education Planning

1. 08:00 - 09:00 : Subject 1 (e.g., Mathematics)
   - Review previous concepts
   - Practice problem-solving

2. 09:15 - 10:15 : Subject 2 (e.g., Science)
   - Read textbook/notes
   - Conduct experiments or watch educational videos

3. 10:30 - 11:30 : Subject 3 (e.g., Language Learning)
   - Grammar and vocabulary exercises
   - Speaking and listening practice

4. 14:00 - 15:00 : Subject 4 (e.g., History or Social Studies)
   - Read chapters or articles
   - Take notes and summarize

5. 15:15 - 16:15 : Skill Development (e.g., Coding, Art, Music)
   - Work on a project or practice new skills
   - Reflect on progress and set goals for tomorrow

Tips:
- Take short breaks between sessions.
- Adjust subjects based on personal goals or weak areas.
- Review and plan each evening for the next day.
